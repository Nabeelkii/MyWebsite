System Engineering Project

The 2D Printer Pen

The 2D printer pen is capable of printing a wide range of high-resolution images and designs directly onto beverages. These pens are intended to be used by cafes and restaurants to attract more customers.

This project aims to reach out to kids to enhance their learning. Before this project, there is no feature or functionality in the current app that converts speech to text. The team saw this as an opportunity for kids to be able to speak and to take that as input into the app and print it out. By doing this, they will be able to visually see the link between the word and the physical object. Below are more details on how the team achieved this.

Details of the project

Component Purpose

Nima17 DC motor Move the Printpen back and forth to print on the food
L298N Motor Driver Connected to the Raspberry Pi to drive the DC motor
Speaker To sound out the word that is being printed letter by letter
To indicate when to start speaking
To sound an alarm before the motor starts
